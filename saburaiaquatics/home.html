Saburai Aquatics will be vending at the Fall 2019 Charlotte Area Aquarium Trade Show (CAATS) on September 21 from 10AM - 4PM! Come support local businesses and meet the team! CAATS is a local production hosted by the Charlotte Area Aquarists Society (see logo) and Missing Links Aquatics Community! Two local fish clubs come together to host the largest aquarium trade show in Charlotte! Saburai Aquatics is proud to take part in the inaugural event, and we hope that you will all come by and be a part of history!

There will be raffles, door prizes, food, fish, plants, equipment, tanks, dry good, driftwood, and MORE!! Don't miss out!
