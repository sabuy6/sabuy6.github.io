Website Topic Approval

What is the purpose of this site?

The purpose of this site is to showcase and sell aquarium livestock.

What do you want the web site to accomplish?

I want this website to be a viable platform for selling fish that I am breeding.

What opportunity, problem, or issue is your site addressing?

I have limited avenues for selling fish, but I have excess stock. I hope to be able to host photos and videos that will give customers a better understanding of what I have to offer.

Who is your intended audience?

Anyone that is interested in purchasing high quality fish for their aquariums.

What type of content might be included in your site?

The website will have a showcase page that has pictures of my tanks and breeders. There will be a sales page. There will be an about me page. There will be a contact page that provides business email, phone, and social media.
