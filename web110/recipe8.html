Gyozas

Ingredients
Filling
Ground Pork 2 pounds
Nira (Garlic Chives) 1 large bunch
Nappa Cabbage 1 head
Garlic 3 cloves
Ginger 1 finger
Soy Sauce 6 tbsp
Sesame Oil 2 tbsp
Dipping Sauce
Soy Sauce 1/2 cup
Rice Wine Vinegar 1/2 cup
Garlic 4 cloves
Sesame Oil 1 tbsp
Skins
Gyoza Skins 4 packs
Instructions
  • Step 1 - Cut nappa and nira into 1/4" pieces.
  • Step 2 - In a large mixing bowl, add all filling ingredients and mix lightly by hand until all ingredients are incorporated.
  • Step 3 - Place approximately one teaspoon of filling in each gyoza and fold them.
  • Step 4 - Place gyozas frill side up into a hot, oiled, frying pan.
  • Step 5 - Sear for approximately 30 seconds, then add water and cover.
  • Step 6 - Cook on medium for 2-3 minutes, periodically adding water.
