FAQ

Some general fish-keeping facts will be answered in this page. I use a variety of different sources including, but not limited to, personal experience, online forums, print magazines, and Google.

How did you get into fish?
My dad kept goldfish in ponds and tubs outside to eat mosquito larvae. I loved sitting by them and watching them.
What is your favorite fish?
I love corydoras. They have a ton of personality. There are hundreds of different species, and they come in several different colors. I love watching them sift through the sand for food.
How often do you ship out fish?
I ship every Monday.
What is your DOA policy?
For a full refund, pictures of dead fish in original packaging (bags) must be sent to saburai.aquatics@gmail.com within an hour of delivery.
What shipping service do you use?
I generally use USPS, but I can use UPS if requested.
